Natural History

The permanent exhibition of the Natural History Department is divided into exhibitions of the Unit of Botany Presentation and Unit of Vertebrates Zoology. It is shown in the central botanical glazed frame and zoological octagonal glazed frame containing environmentally exhibited displays of vertebrates who inhabit the Republic of Srpska. The botanical exhibition illustrates the variety and beauty of the flora in the Republic of Srpska, rich resources of medicinal flora, status of certain endangered plant species as well as the necessary safety measures in order to preserve the natural heritage. The exhibition shows “Medicinal and Edible Plants of Our Region” and “Endemic, Rare and Endangered Plants of Our Region”. With herbarium specimens, colorful pictures and related text are presented 72 plant species, whereof 24 species are endemic, rare and endangered plants and 48 are medicinal and edible plants. The mycological exhibition contains 52 species of mushrooms whereof 34 types are edible, 2 conditional edible, 8 non-edible and 8 species belong to the group of poisonous mushrooms. With their presentation, visitors can get to know some of the fungi that thrive in our region, which species are edible, conditionally edible or inedible and poisonous, in which habitat they grow and in what period they mature.
