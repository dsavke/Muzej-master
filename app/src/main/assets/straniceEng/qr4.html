The ancient

The ancient part of the permanent exhibition consists of: jewelry, coins, glass and ceramics artefacts, lamps (weevils), toiletries and stone sculptures (funerary and votive monuments). The ancient way of life, not always of the same intensity, lasted practically from the first century until the end of the sixth century and the beginning of VII century, when the Slavs inhabited this region.
