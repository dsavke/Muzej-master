Zoological exhibition

The zoological exhibition shows the most important and characteristic natural habitats that are represented in our country. The following habitats are shown: dry and rocky habitat of sub-Mediterranean and high mountain type (Section 1), coniferous forests (Section 2), deciduous forests (Section 3), trout waters and carp waters (Section 4), streams and ponds with pure running water (Section 5), swamp habitats in the period of propagation (Section 6), in the period of migration (Section 7) and in the period of winter and wandering (Section 8).
